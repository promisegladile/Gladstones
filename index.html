THE IMPACT OF INSTANT MESSAGING IN THE BUISNESS WORLD



Instant messaging is taking over or lives and making it easier for us to communicate more quickly and efficiently with others from where ever we are. The aim of this investigation is to investigate how businesses have adapted and implemented instant messaging in order to improve their businesses and look at some of the negative and positive aspects of instant messaging in the business world on a national level
