Instant messaging software that female Business women use

This pie chart represents the usage of instant messaging software among female business women.
The chart shows that the majority of women, accounting for 60% of the total, use WhatsApp for their
instant messaging needs. Following closely behind is Microsoft Teams with 15% usage, while only 10%
of the women use Wechat. The remaining 15% of women use facebook messanger. Overall, the chart
suggests that WhatsApp is the preferred choice of instant messaging software for female business women.
